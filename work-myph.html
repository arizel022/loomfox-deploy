Work MYPH

The school of conceptual and art photography, MYPH, was founded in 2018 by Sergey Melnitchenko in his hometown, Mykolaiv but quickly gained recognition throughout Ukraine. Currently, the students of the school are artists located both within the country and abroad.

The institution aims to broaden awareness and understanding of contemporary conceptual and artistic photography among photographers. Thus, in theory and in practice, online and offline, even during times of war in Ukraine, Sergey, along with invited lecturers, shares his diverse artistic and commercial experience with students.
