Red Luna

The Red Luna app is your personal librarian, guide, archivist, and analyst, seamlessly integrating with various music services like YouTube Music, Apple Music, Spotify, SoundCloud, and many more. Boom gathers information from all these sources and presents it to you in a user-friendly format, providing insightful analysis of your listening habits by genre, artist, track, and year.

What i did

  • Developed a business concept for the application and its individual functions.
  • Developed the visual style of the application and Red Luna brand.
  • Created the design of the entire application, as well as individual functionality

While the Red Luna project currently exists only as a business and visual concept, it showcases a compelling business logic with well-structured visualizations. The app's modern design and user-centric approach make it an attractive proposition for music lovers, with clear commercial potential.
